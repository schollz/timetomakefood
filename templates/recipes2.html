About

timetomakefood.com is an educational website to help inform where food comes from and how food is made. The amount of time and effort that goes into modern food is often lost on the consumer, so timetomakefood.com helps you to explore the food tree to its most central components.

 

Read more about the inspiration for this site at rpiai.com.
